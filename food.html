Ranch Chicken Wraps

This recipe uses

Original Almond Breeze

View Product

Directions

  • In a large skillet over medium heat, add oil.

    Season chicken with salt, pepper and garlic powder, then place in skillet.

    Cook until browned and an inter`nal temperature reaches 165°F.

  • Add broth, almond and oat blend and ranch seasoning mix.

    Bring to a simmer, then stir in cornstarch slurry and cook until thickened.

  • Place chicken in tortillas and top with lettuce, avocado, red onion and tomato.

  • Fold tightly and serve. Refrigerate leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil
  • ½ tsp. salt½ tsp. pepper
  • ½ tsp. garlic powder
  • ¼ cup chicken broth
  • ½ cup Original Almond & Oat Blend
  • 1 (1 oz.) packet ranch seasoning mix
  • 1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 Tbsp. cornstarch dissolved in ¼ cup water
  • 4 (10”) flour tortillas
  • 2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • ½ cup red onion, sliced
  • 1 Roma tomato, chopped
